## THE ONE CRITICAL INSIGHT
> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Selling "raw scraped data" is a commoditized dead-end. The money is in "Clean, Human-Verified Data + Zero CAC outreach."**
> Anyone can scrape Google Maps using free tools, but clients are frustrated by "unqualified nonsense" and high email bounce rates. You can completely win this market by using Antigravity to build a custom validation script, offering **50 free verified leads** as a sales hook, and using **Cold Email** (your own service) to acquire clients with **$0 CAC**, completely bypassing the expensive Google Ads market.

## RECOMMENDED STRATEGY (CONDITIONAL GO)

### Phase 1: Set Up the AI Scraper & Validation Pipeline
1. Create a script utilizing Google Maps API (or Outscraper API) + Email enrichment.
2. Build an automated cleaning and verification pipeline (filtering duplicates, running contacts through NeverBounce/ZeroBounce API to guarantee < 2% bounce rate).

### Phase 2: The "Value-First" Outreach ($0 CAC)
1. Identify 100 potential B2B agency clients (web designers, SEO agencies, local ads agencies).
2. For each, scrape 50 highly targeted local business leads matching their target client profile (e.g., HVAC companies or dentists with poor ratings).
3. Send a cold email: *"Hey [Name], I noticed you sell web design to contractors. I scraped 50 local plumbers in your area who don't have websites, verified their phone and email, and attached them to this email for free. If you like the quality, I can build you a custom list of 1,000 leads for $150."*

### Phase 3: Upsell to Monthly Retainer
- Offer a subscription model: **"1,500 new verified leads/month for $199/mo"** to lock in recurring revenue.

## OVERALL VERDICT
### 🟢 CONDITIONAL GO
* **Go if**: You acquire clients via cold email ($0 CAC) using free samples as a hook, and deliver **verified** leads (not raw junk).
* **No-Go if**: You try to sell generic raw directories or buy Google Ads (unprofitable unit economics).
