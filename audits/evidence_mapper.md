# Contender: EvidenceMapper (Round 4 Winner)

## 1. Core Problem
Shopify merchants selling subscriptions face a high rate of chargebacks ("I didn't order this"). Losing a chargeback means losing the product, refunding the cash, AND paying a $15 Stripe penalty. Too many chargebacks and Stripe shuts down their merchant account. Fighting chargebacks manually takes hours of compiling PDFs.

## 2. The Solution
An automated Shopify App. When a chargeback occurs, EvidenceMapper automatically pulls tracking data from Shopify, customer IP, order history, and formats it into a bank-compliant PDF. It then uses Stripe API to automatically submit the evidence. 100% hands-off for the merchant.

## 3. The True Moat
**Operational Lock-in (Set-and-Forget).** Once the merchant connects their Shopify and Stripe accounts to EvidenceMapper and it successfully wins their first dispute, they will NEVER uninstall it. It acts as an invisible insurance policy running in the background.

## 4. The Market Proof
Competitors like **Chargeflow** and **Disputifier** exist and make millions. However, they charge a "percentage of recovered revenue" (often 20-25%). Merchants hate giving away their revenue.
**Our Angle:** Fixed price SaaS (e.g., $99/mo). Huge merchants will flock to us to avoid percentage fees.

## 5. $0 CAC Strategy
1. **Shopify App Store SEO:** Rank for "chargeback protection", "dispute manager". Organic, high-intent traffic.
2. **Engineering as Marketing:** A free web tool "Shopify Chargeback Penalty Risk Calculator". Distributed in e-commerce Facebook groups. Merchants calculate their risk, get scared, and click "Install EvidenceMapper to fix this".

## 6. Target User & Willingness to Pay
- **User:** Shopify Subscription Merchants.
- **Pain Level:** 10/10 (literally losing cash and risking account suspension).
- **Willingness to pay:** $99 - $149/mo (cheaper than paying an employee to fight disputes, cheaper than competitor percentage fees).
