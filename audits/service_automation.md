### Exact Service Description

**"Done-For-You Business Automation Setup"**

The client gets:
- **Discovery questionnaire** (what apps they use, what manual work they want to eliminate)
- **Custom automation workflow** built and configured in their Zapier or Make.com account
- **Workflow documentation** (what it does, how to modify it, troubleshooting guide)
- **Testing & validation** (proof it works with real data)
- **30-day support window** for bug fixes

**Productized packages:**

| Package | What's Included | Our Price | Competitor Avg |
|---------|----------------|-----------|----------------|
| **Starter** | 1 workflow, 2 apps connected, basic trigger→action | $297 | $100–$300 (Fiverr) |
| **Growth** | 3 workflows, multi-app, conditional logic, error handling | $797 | $500–$1,500 (Upwork) |
| **Enterprise** | 5+ workflows, full business process automation, API webhooks | $1,497 | $2,000–$10,000 (Agencies) |

### Why the Client Does NOT Care Who Does the Work (Zero Trust Barrier)

✅ **They're buying an outcome, not a relationship.** The client wants their lead form connected to their CRM — period. They don't care if it's built by a human in the Philippines, a freelancer in Germany, or an AI agent.

✅ **It's a one-time setup.** Unlike design or branding (where taste/style matters), automation is binary: it either works or it doesn't. There's no subjective judgment.

✅ **The platform IS the trust.** Zapier/Make.com are trusted brands. The client's data stays in their own accounts. We're just configuring their tools.

✅ **Documentation replaces the person.** A well-documented workflow with a visual flow diagram is self-explanatory. The client never needs to talk to "the developer" again.

✅ **Low stakes for first purchase.** A $297–$797 project is low-risk enough that clients will try an unknown provider (especially with a money-back guarantee).

**Free Lead Magnet / Portfolio Piece:**

>
> 📋 **Scenario:** When a new lead submits a Typeform/Google Form:
> 1. ✅ Lead data is instantly added to a Google Sheet (master lead tracker)
> 2. ✅ A personalized welcome email is sent via Gmail within 60 seconds
> 3. ✅ A Slack notification fires to the #sales channel with lead details
> 4. ✅ If the lead selected "Enterprise" as company size, a high-priority tag is added
>
> **Deliverable:** Loom video walkthrough (2 min) showing the live workflow running + screenshot of the Make.com/Zapier scenario canvas + documentation PDF.

This sample can be built once and reused across all marketing materials.

### How Antigravity AI Fulfills This 100%

## 🏆 SUB-NICHE #2: CRO Audit Reports

### Exact Service Description

**"Comprehensive Conversion Rate Optimization Audit"**

The client gets:
- **Full website conversion audit** (20–40 page PDF report)
- **Google Analytics data analysis** (conversion funnels, drop-off points, traffic quality)
- **Page speed & Core Web Vitals assessment** (with specific fix recommendations)
- **Mobile usability review** (screenshots + issues identified)
- **UX friction point analysis** (navigation, CTAs, forms, trust signals)
- **Competitor comparison** (2–3 competitor sites benchmarked)
- **Prioritized action plan** (ICE-scored: Impact × Confidence × Ease)
- **A/B test hypotheses** (5–10 ready-to-run test ideas with expected outcomes)

**Productized packages:**

| Package | What's Included | Our Price | Competitor Avg |
|---------|----------------|-----------|----------------|
| **Quick Scan** | 5-page audit, top 10 issues, quick wins only | $497 | $500–$990 (Freelancers) |
| **Full Audit** | 20+ page report, full funnel analysis, prioritized roadmap | $1,497 | $1,500–$3,500 (Agencies) |
| **Enterprise Audit** | 40+ pages, competitor benchmarking, A/B test plan, stakeholder deck | $2,997 | $3,500–$5,500 (Agencies) |

### Why the Client Does NOT Care Who Does the Work (Zero Trust Barrier)

✅ **They're buying a DOCUMENT.** The deliverable is a PDF report. It either contains actionable insights or it doesn't. The client will never meet or know the analyst.

✅ **Data speaks for itself.** The audit is based on objective metrics — page speed scores, bounce rates, heatmap patterns. There's no "creative vision" involved.

✅ **Agencies already outsource this.** Many CRO agencies use junior analysts, contractors, or even automated tools (Hotjar, Crazy Egg, PageSpeed Insights) to compile their audits. Clients know this. They pay for the ANALYSIS, not the analyst.

✅ **Lower-end audits are literally checklists.** Research confirms that sub-$1,500 audits often rely heavily on "best practices checklists" — which is exactly what an AI excels at, but better and more comprehensive.

✅ **One-time engagement.** No ongoing relationship needed. Client gets report, implements changes, done.

### What a "Working Sample" Would Look Like

**Free Lead Magnet / Portfolio Piece:**

> **"Sample CRO Audit: [Example E-commerce Store]" — PDF Download**
>
> 📊 Contents:
> 1. Executive Summary (key findings in 1 page)
> 2. PageSpeed Insights analysis (mobile + desktop scores, specific bottlenecks)
> 3. Homepage UX teardown (annotated screenshots — CTA visibility, trust signals, above-the-fold content)
> 4. Checkout funnel analysis (identified 3 drop-off friction points)
> 5. Mobile responsiveness issues (4 specific bugs found)
> 6. Top 10 prioritized recommendations (ICE-scored)
> 7. 3 ready-to-test A/B hypotheses
>
> **Format:** Professionally designed PDF (15 pages), branded with Antigravity AI logo.

Audit a publicly visible e-commerce site (e.g., a well-known DTC brand) and publish the sample. This immediately demonstrates competence.

### How Antigravity AI Fulfills This 100%

| Fulfillment Step | AI Capability |
|-----------------|---------------|
| **PageSpeed analysis** | AI calls Google PageSpeed Insights API, parses results, generates recommendations |
| **Core Web Vitals** | AI pulls CrUX data or Lighthouse reports, interprets scores |
| **Mobile usability** | AI uses responsive testing tools/APIs, identifies breakpoints and issues |
| **UX heuristic evaluation** | AI applies Nielsen's 10 usability heuristics systematically to each page |
| **CTA & trust signal review** | AI analyzes page structure, identifies missing trust elements, weak CTAs |
| **Funnel analysis** | AI analyzes provided GA data (or public data) to map drop-off points |
| **Competitor benchmarking** | AI scrapes competitor sites, compares page speed, structure, trust elements |
| **Recommendations + ICE scoring** | AI generates prioritized action items with impact/confidence/ease scores |
| **A/B test hypotheses** | AI writes structured hypotheses: "Changing X to Y will increase Z because W" |
| **Report generation** | AI compiles all findings into professional Markdown → PDF with charts and screenshots |

> **Key insight:** A CRO audit is fundamentally a structured analysis of publicly available data (page speed, site structure, UX patterns) combined with established best practices. This is precisely what LLMs excel at — pattern matching against known frameworks, systematic evaluation, and structured report generation.
