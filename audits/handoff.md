# Contender 3: Handoff.so (Freelancer Asset Vault)

## 1. Core Problem
High-income freelancers (Graphic Designers, Web Developers, Consultants) suffer from "Professionalism-to-Payment" friction. They finish a project, email the final deliverables (.ZIP, .PDF, .FIG), and send the final invoice. The client downloads the files but delays payment by 30+ days or demands endless "free tweaks" because the freelancer has lost all leverage.

## 2. The Solution
**Handoff.so** is a secure Asset Vault & Sign-Off Engine. Freelancers upload their final deliverables to the vault instead of emailing them. The client receives a secure link where they can preview a watermarked version or read a description of the deliverable. To click the "Download/Unlock" button, the client is forced to pay the final Stripe invoice directly on that page. Payment unlocks the file automatically.

## 3. The True Moat
**Immediate Leverage & High Switching Cost.** It solves a massive emotional and financial pain point instantly. Once a freelancer gets used to getting paid upfront before the file unlocks, they will never go back to sending attachments via Gmail.

## 4. $0 CAC Strategy (Ecosystem Parasite)
**The B2B Viral Loop.** 
The product inherently markets itself. When Freelancer A sends a Handoff link to Client B (who is also a business owner), Client B interacts with the beautifully designed payment wall. After paying, Client B sees a subtle "Powered by Handoff - Stop chasing unpaid invoices from your clients. Use this." Client B signs up. 

## 5. Target User & Willingness to Pay
- **User (The Supplier):** Prosumers / Freelancers / Solo-Agencies.
- **Buyer (The Payer):** The Freelancer's client.
- **Monetization:** Freelancer pays RM40/mo subscription OR we take a 2% platform fee on all cleared transactions.
- **Pain Level:** 10/10 (Direct cash flow bottleneck).
