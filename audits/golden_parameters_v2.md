# THE GOLDEN PARAMETERS V2.0
*(The Solo Micro-SaaS Manifesto)*

These constraints are ABSOLUTE. Any SaaS idea that fails even one of these parameters must be rejected immediately.

## 1. Target Market: "Micro-B2B" & "Prosumer" Only (Zero Enterprise, Zero Pure B2C)
- **MUST:** Sell to Small Businesses, E-Commerce Stores (Shopify), Solo-Agencies, Freelancers, or the Prosumer/Creator Economy (individuals who use your software to MAKE money).
- **MUST NOT:** Sell to large Enterprises or Government (B2G). As a "Nobody" solo dev, you cannot afford the 6-month sales cycles, security audits, and trust barriers required for enterprise procurement.
- **MUST NOT:** Build Pure B2C apps (Diet trackers, to-do lists, alarm clocks) where the user pays out of pocket for "convenience" rather than "financial return".

## 2. Sales Cycle: "Zero-Touch" (Zero Meetings)
- **MUST:** The buyer must be able to visit the landing page, understand the value in 30 seconds, click "Install", and pay via Stripe automatically at 3 AM.
- **MUST NOT:** Require Zoom meetings, manual invoicing, Proof of Concepts (POCs), or board approvals to close a sale.

## 3. Acquisition: Ecosystem Parasite ($0 CAC)
- **MUST:** The software must live where the buyers are already searching with high intent (e.g., Shopify App Store, Chrome Web Store, specific developer forums, or niche integrations marketplaces).
- **MUST NOT:** Rely on paid Facebook/Google Ads, cold calling, or building organic traffic from zero.

## 4. Time-to-Value: Immediate ROI
- **MUST:** Within 1 hour of installing, the user must see the software either save them money or make them money.
- **MUST NOT:** Require 6 months of data gathering (like DePIN networks) or wait for a massive network effect before the product becomes useful.

## 5. Architecture: Single-Sided & Headless
- **MUST:** Single-sided tool. Background API integrations, automated workflows, or "Set and Forget" headless utilities.
- **MUST NOT:** Be a two-sided marketplace. No finding vendors AND buyers. No physical world logistics.

## 6. Defensibility: Workflow Lock-in (God-Tier Moat)
- **MUST:** The cost of uninstalling your software must be high. Once embedded into their workflow (e.g., automated billing, automatic chargeback fighting), removing it breaks their operations or costs them direct cash.
- **MUST NOT:** Be a simple "ChatGPT Wrapper" or basic UI layer that an intern can clone in a weekend.
