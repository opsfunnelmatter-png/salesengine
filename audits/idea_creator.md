# God-Tier Micro-SaaS: SponsorSweep.io

## The Core Inefficiency (Time & Money Drain)
YouTubers, TikTokers, and Podcasters sign finite sponsorship deals (e.g., 30 to 60 days of active tracking). They embed the brand's tracking link in their video descriptions, show notes, and pinned comments. After the campaign ends, the video continues to accumulate passive views for years. 

Because of this, the creator is unknowingly giving away free, highly-targeted traffic to the brand long after they've stopped being paid. Worse, these custom sponsor links often expire, resulting in 404 errors. Manually hunting through a backlog of 300+ videos to swap out expired sponsor links for active, monetizable affiliate links is a soul-crushing, unscalable chore that creators simply ignore—leaving thousands of dollars in residual income on the table.

## The Micro-SaaS Solution ($29 - $49/mo)
**SponsorSweep** is a YouTube API & Smart-Link platform built explicitly for post-sponsorship catalog monetization.

1. **Time-Delayed Smart Links:** Creators generate a `sponsorsweep.link/brand` URL for their new video. They set the contract's expiration date (e.g., Day 60). On Day 61, the link automatically shifts away from the sponsor's destination and redirects all future traffic to the creator's evergreen Amazon Storefront, Patreon, or an active affiliate offer.
2. **Bulk Catalog Sweeper:** The creator connects their channel via YouTube OAuth. SponsorSweep instantly scans their entire video back-catalog, flags 404 links, identifies expired sponsor domains, and allows the creator to bulk-update or auto-replace them in one click.

## Why it hits the "God-Tier" Golden Parameters

* **Single-Sided & No Marketplace:** It is a pure utility tool. It requires zero network effects, no brand-side portal, and no complex onboarding.
* **Immediate ROI:** The value proposition is tied directly to recovered revenue. If a creator recovers 5,000 clicks a month and points them to a $2 CPA affiliate offer, they instantly make $100. The $29/mo subscription pays for itself on day one.
* **Extreme Retention:** It operates as a "set-and-forget" infrastructure. Once a creator's links are routed through SponsorSweep, cancelling the software means abandoning their automated revenue. Churn will be virtually non-existent.
* **Self-Serve:** Creators authorize YouTube, set their default fallback link (e.g., their Amazon Storefront), and the software does the rest.

## $0 CAC Distribution Strategy
**The "Dead Money" Free Audit**

You build a free, ungated web application (or Chrome Extension) where a creator inputs their YouTube channel handle. The tool aggressively scans their 50 most-viewed videos, checks the HTTP status of every link in the descriptions, and estimates traffic using YouTube view-velocity data. 

It then generates a highly shareable **"Revenue Leak Score"**:
> *"🚨 We found 18 dead 404 links and 34 expired sponsor links in your top videos. These links are currently getting ~12,000 clicks a month. You are losing an estimated $450/month in affiliate revenue."*

To instantly bulk-fix the errors and reroute the dead links to their own store, they just click "Fix My Channel" and upgrade to the $29/mo tier. The problem is agitated perfectly, and the solution is one click away, creating a near-perfect conversion loop. Creators will naturally share their "Leak Scores" in Discord communities and Twitter, driving organic viral growth.
