# THE GOLDEN PARAMETERS V3.0
*(The AI Drop-Servicing / Arbitrage Manifesto)*

This model shifts the founder from "Software Builder" to "Traffic Arbitrageur". The founder buys high-intent traffic, sells a high-ticket service, and the AI (Antigravity) fulfills the work.

These are the strict constraints for selecting a service to sell:

## 1. Traffic & Acquisition (High-Intent Ads)
- **MUST:** Rely on Google Ads targeting "Bottom-of-Funnel" keywords (e.g., "Hire Next.js expert", "Fix Shopify speed now"). The client is actively holding their credit card looking for a solution *today*.
- **MUST NOT:** Rely on building organic social media, content marketing, or slow SEO. We buy attention and convert it immediately.

## 2. Market & Currency (Geo-Arbitrage)
- **MUST:** Target high-currency tier 1 countries (USA, UK, Australia, Singapore). A $500 (RM2,300) invoice is cheap to an American business, but highly profitable for us.
- **MUST NOT:** Target local/low-currency markets where businesses haggle over RM100.

## 3. Trust Barrier (Zero Track-Record Closing)
- **MUST:** Be a "Transactional" or "Performance-Based" service. The client pays for the *mathematical outcome* (e.g., data delivered, speed score turned green, API connected). They do not care about your portfolio or testimonials if you offer a free sample or a "pay-on-results" guarantee.
- **MUST NOT:** Sell services that require high emotional trust, subjective design taste (like Logo Design), or long-term consulting where they demand to see your university degree.

## 4. Fulfillment (100% AI Execution)
- **MUST:** The service must be 100% executable by an elite Coding/Data AI agent (Antigravity) within hours. Examples: Writing Python scrapers, optimizing React code, writing API middlewares, generating database migrations.
- **MUST NOT:** Require physical labor, manual video editing, human phone calls, or subjective creative writing.

## 5. The Founder's Role
- **MUST:** The founder ONLY does two things: (1) Manages the Google Ads budget/Landing Page, and (2) Forwards the client's requirements to the AI, then forwards the AI's output back to the client.
- **MUST NOT:** Do the technical heavy lifting or get bogged down in coding the deliverables themselves.
