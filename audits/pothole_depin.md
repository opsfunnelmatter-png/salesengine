# Contender: Pothole DePIN (Round 8 Winner - Passive Data Infrastructure)

## 1. Core Problem
Logistics fleets (J&T, NinjaVan, DHL) and autonomous vehicle companies suffer millions in vehicle maintenance and damaged high-value goods (fragile electronics) due to bad road conditions and potholes. Standard GPS APIs (like Google Maps) optimize for *time* and *distance*, but they completely lack data on physical *road roughness*. 

## 2. The Solution
A "Road Roughness API". We crowdsource accelerometer data (Z-axis bounces) paired with GPS coordinates from thousands of everyday drivers to create a real-time, hyper-accurate heat map of potholes, speed bumps, and degraded roads. Enterprises subscribe to our API to route their fragile cargo or expensive fleets away from high-vibration routes.

## 3. The True Moat
**Passive Proprietary Data Aggregation.** 
This is a software-only DePIN (Decentralized Physical Infrastructure Network). Once the network has enough nodes (phones driving around), the data asset becomes a monopoly. Competitors cannot replicate the data without building an identically massive, actively driving user base.

## 4. The Market Proof
Startups like *Carmera* and *Nexar* (which use dashcams) prove there is massive enterprise appetite for physical-world data. However, dashcams require physical hardware. By using smartphone accelerometers, we drop the hardware cost to zero.

## 5. $0 CAC Strategy (How to get nodes)
**The Trojan Utility App.**
We do NOT ask people to "download our pothole app" (nobody cares about that). 
Instead, we launch a **Free Mileage & Tax Tracker for Uber/Grab/Lalamove Drivers**. These high-mileage drivers desperately need tools to track their deductible miles for tax seasons. While our app runs in the background logging their GPS tracks for their taxes, it *passively* mines the accelerometer data. We instantly acquire thousands of high-mileage drivers mapping the city for us daily for free.

## 6. Target User & Willingness to Pay
- **User (The Supplier):** Gig drivers (Using the app for free utility).
- **Buyer (The Enterprise):** Logistics fleets, Auto Insurance companies.
- **Pain Level (Buyer):** 8/10 (High fleet maintenance costs / delayed claims).
- **Willingness to pay:** Tiered API access ($500 - $5,000+ per month per enterprise depending on volume).
