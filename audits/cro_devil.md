## TECHNICAL DELIVERY RISKS
- Risk 1: Flawed Analytics Setup: If the client's GA4 or click events are improperly tracked or duplicate-tagged, the audit's findings will be based on corrupted data and lead to false recommendations.
- Risk 2: Insufficient Statistical Significance: Without a baseline of at least 10,000 monthly unique visitors, executing validation tests on audit hypotheses is impossible, leaving you with unproven theories.
- Risk 3: Client Implementation Failure: Most clients lack internal design and development resources to execute audit recommendations, causing the audit report to sit unused while you get blamed for zero revenue lift.

## TRUST BARRIER ANALYSIS
- Can a no-portfolio founder close clients at $500+? NO
- Evidence: Clients view CRO as an investment to recover lost revenue and expect case studies showing exact ROI percentages. Cold-pitching a premium diagnostic service without a track record is rejected as a high-risk gamble (Source: reddit.com).

## FINAL VERDICT
- Viability score for solo no-portfolio founder: 2/10
- Dead-end or viable with modifications: Viable with modifications
- If viable, what modification is needed: The founder must stop selling standalone "audits." Instead, they should offer a free 5-minute Loom video teardown of one high-friction page element to build trust, collect 3 case studies, and transition to a bundle that includes both the audit and its technical implementation (design, copy writing, and web builds).
