## TECHNICAL DELIVERY RISKS
- Risk 1: **Constant Data Decay.** Contact details decay at over 2% per month, meaning delivered lists quickly bounce, leading to refund demands.
- Risk 2: **Scraper Detection and IP Blocking.** Google Maps and LinkedIn actively block scrapers, resulting in IP bans, captchas, and script breakages.
- Risk 3: **Email Deliverability Failure.** Lack of technical setup (DKIM/SPF/DMARC) causes outreach to go straight to spam, delivering zero results.

## TRUST BARRIER ANALYSIS
- Can a no-portfolio founder close clients at $500+? NO
- Evidence: Clients view list building as a low-value commodity and will not pay $500+ to someone without case studies or proven outreach success. They will opt for cheap offshore freelancers or use self-serve AI platforms instead.

## FINAL VERDICT
- Viability score for solo no-portfolio founder: 2/10
- Dead-end or viable with modifications: Viable with modifications
- If viable, what modification is needed: Transition to a "Managed Outcome" model (booking meetings, setting up cold email infrastructure) rather than selling raw list spreadsheets, and offer a custom, hand-verified 50-lead sample list during outreach to prove capability upfront.
