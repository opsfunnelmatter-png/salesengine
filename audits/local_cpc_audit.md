### Competitor Strategy Takeaways
1. **Positioning:** Budget competitors (RM150 - RM500) sell "pages." Mid-tier to premium competitors (RM1,500+) sell "results," "sales funnels," and "leads."
2. **Ad Tactics:** Competitors running ads rarely advertise "cheap website design." Instead, they pitch "Get More Customers," "Increase Sales by 300%," or "Lead Generation Funnels."
3. **Upsell Strategy:** Almost all competitors start with a baseline design price and charge extra for:
   * Professional copywriting (RM200 - RM500 extra)
   * Payment Gateway Integration (RM150 - RM300 extra)
   * Annual domain/hosting renewal fees (typically RM150 - RM350/year after Year 1)

## 3. Unit Economics Calculations for a RM999 Offer

The proposed offer is a **RM999 Landing Page Package** (e.g., includes custom landing page, basic copywriting, Google Analytics/pixel setup, and 1 year of domain + hosting).

### Baseline Assumptions
* **Offer Price:** RM 999.00
* **COGS (Cost of Goods Sold):** RM 150.00 (Includes .com/.my domain registration, basic hosting allocation, and premium theme/plugin licensing costs)
* **Gross Margin:** RM 849.00 (Gross Margin % = 85.0%)

---

### Model A: Lead Generation + WhatsApp/Sales Call Close
In this model, the landing page is designed to capture leads (e.g., via a WhatsApp button click or a lead form submission), and a salesperson closes the deal manually.

#### 1. Conservative Scenario (High CPC, Low CVR, Low Close Rate)
* **CPC:** RM 4.00
* **Lead Conversion Rate (CVR):** 3.0%
* **Cost Per Lead (CPL):** $\frac{\text{CPC}}{\text{Lead CVR}} = \frac{\text{RM } 4.00}{0.03} = \text{RM } 133.33$
* **Close Rate:** 10.0%
* **Leads Needed per Sale:** 10 leads
* **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC):** $\text{CPL} \times 10 = \text{RM } 1,333.33$
* **Net Profit per Sale:** $\text{Price} - \text{CAC} - \text{COGS} = \text{RM } 999 - \text{RM } 1,333.33 - \text{RM } 150 = -\text{RM } 484.33$
* **ROAS (Return on Ad Spend):** $\frac{\text{Price}}{\text{CAC}} = \mathbf{0.75x}$
* **Status:** **UNPROFITABLE**

#### 2. Moderate Scenario (Average CPC, Standard CVR, Average Close Rate)
* **CPC:** RM 2.00
* **Lead Conversion Rate (CVR):** 5.0%
* **Cost Per Lead (CPL):** $\frac{\text{CPC}}{\text{Lead CVR}} = \frac{\text{RM } 2.00}{0.05} = \text{RM } 40.00$
* **Close Rate:** 20.0%
* **Leads Needed per Sale:** 5 leads
* **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC):** $\text{CPL} \times 5 = \text{RM } 200.00$
* **Net Profit per Sale:** $\text{Price} - \text{CAC} - \text{COGS} = \text{RM } 999 - \text{RM } 200 - \text{RM } 150 = \mathbf{\text{RM } 649.00}$
* **ROAS (Return on Ad Spend):** $\frac{\text{Price}}{\text{CAC}} = \mathbf{5.00x}$
* **Status:** **HIGHLY PROFITABLE**

#### 3. Optimistic Scenario (Low CPC, High CVR, High Close Rate)
* **CPC:** RM 1.20 (Using localized long-tail Malay keywords)
* **Lead Conversion Rate (CVR):** 8.0%
* **Cost Per Lead (CPL):** $\frac{\text{CPC}}{\text{Lead CVR}} = \frac{\text{RM } 1.20}{0.08} = \text{RM } 15.00$
* **Close Rate:** 30.0%
* **Leads Needed per Sale:** 3.33 leads
* **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC):** $\text{CPL} \times 3.33 = \text{RM } 50.00$
* **Net Profit per Sale:** $\text{Price} - \text{CAC} - \text{COGS} = \text{RM } 999 - \text{RM } 50 - \text{RM } 150 = \mathbf{\text{RM } 799.00}$
* **ROAS (Return on Ad Spend):** $\frac{\text{Price}}{\text{CAC}} = \mathbf{19.98x}$
* **Status:** **EXTREMELY PROFITABLE**

## 4. Key Recommendations

1. **Target Malay Search Queries First:** 
   Keywords like `"servis buat landing page"` and `"buat landing page murah"` have a much lower CPC (RM0.80 - RM1.80) compared to English corporate terms like `"landing page developer"` or `"web design agency KL"` (RM3.00 - RM8.00). Target small business owners/SMEs using these localized terms to minimize CAC.
   
2. **Optimize for Lead Gen CVR (WhatsApp Close):**
   Selling a RM999 digital service directly through checkout (Model B) is difficult without high brand authority. A WhatsApp-based close model (Model A) is highly recommended for the Malaysian market, where business owners prefer to chat before paying. This also allows for upselling maintenance packages or ad management services.
   
3. **Offer Speed Optimization as a USP:**
   Very few budget competitors (under RM1,000) offer speed optimization. By guaranteeing a Google PageSpeed score of >90, you can differentiate the RM999 offer from competitors like TukangWeb or Sistem Niaga, who offer cheap template pages without performance optimizations.
